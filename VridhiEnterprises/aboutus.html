ABOUT US

Our Vision

Vridhi Enterprises is a leading manufacturer of Ready-To-Cook and Ready-To-Eat range of products for Hotels, Restaurants and Catering Service providers. Our products are for Commercial Kitchens as well as our Brand TAMY’S for home makers is a boon which makes cooking easy and saves time. We provide all the products in customized in bulk packaging which will serve you delicious dishes in both the segments of Ready-To-Eat and Ready-To-Cook. Our 1 kg packet of curry paste can produce 2 kg of gravy for you, making it cost effective. Our clients list includes esteemed hotels, restaurants, big caterers and specially, QSR (Quick Serve Restaurants) from all over the world. Our traditional way of manufacturing gravy adds up authentic taste to the products. Our few range of specialized gravies make us most preferred manufacturers in HORECA segment. We are always committed to serve the best and authentic products.
